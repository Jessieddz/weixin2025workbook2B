Inspiration

This sketch is a combination of fonts, shapes, colors, and transformations. As the mouse moves, the pink cherry blossom surrounding the central font moves rapidly, from one whole to two individual circles. In this visual process, the "me" maintains coordinates in the center of the elements and rotates in place, not controlled by the mouse, thus maintaining the balance of readability and visual beauty.

me & you, then us

Through the movement of the mouse, the page shows the continuous iteration of words that disappear and reappear like bubbles, and in this process, the mouse touch will produce two different sounds similar to the technology of bubbles. In addition, the user can click the specified keys on the keyboard to change the color and type of the font.
